Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Practice Did Not Receive Lab Results for One or More Patients

Cause: Not Applicable
Solution: The practice has been receiving lab reports for some patients, but data is missing for one or more patients.

Details:
  1. Get the patient name, lab/test name and date from the practice. If practice has more than one lab interface ask them which lab company sent the result.
  2. In CMA, go the Customer Information page for the practice.
  3. Scroll down to the "Messages" section and click on "Advanced Search".
     
  4. Type in the patient"s last name.  You don"t have to type in the patient"s first name but if you do -- use this format: last name, first name.  Click the "Search" button.  The Message Status and Purpose fields can remain blank.  
  5. Locate the patient or patients you need.  Look in the "From" column for the name of the corresponding lab company who sent the result then click on the magnify glass to the far left to verify this is the result needing to be resent. 
    NOTE: If you can"t find the particular patient you are looking for with advanced search or in the message section without using "Advanced Search" and/or by looking on the individual pages by date, then the practice will need to contact the lab company for them to resend that patients labs and after they do, the results should automatically transfer to the practice through CMA.
     
  6. Now, look under the "Key" column then find "Description" and to the right under the "Info" column will be the name of the lab/test.  Note:  Sometimes you might have to click on the first "download" and search in the actual HL7 message because there may be multiple results in the HL7.
     
  7. Click on your browsers "Back" button to return to the "Message" screen and click in the white box to the far right of the row of the message/result you are going to resend and then click on the   (Symbol is of a blue circle with arrow pointing counter-clockwise located atop all the white boxes column) to resend the message.  
  8. An Internet Explorer message will pop up asking if you sure and if you are click "Ok", screen will refresh and message status should changed to "Received" then eventually "Complete" within two minutes.
