How to: Troubleshoot Lab Integration Problems.

Solution:
Details:

What happens if the user ordering the lab does not select the lab on the Refer Patient for Treatment screen?

The lab will still appear on the Outstanding Labs and Tests list. When results are received, and are matched to the lab, the lab will then appear on the Daily Lab Results screen for the appropriate lab facility and date.

What happens to the results if the lab is not ordered during the encounter?

When the system cannot match the results to a lab ordered for the patient, as in this case, the results file is saved to the To Be Posted list. When a user manually posts the results file, the appropriate lab is created and it will then appear on the Daily Lab Results screen for the appropriate lab facility and date.
