Description
The Care Team is a patient-specific short list that is dynamically built from consultants and providers that have interacted with this patient in some manner. Providers who have ordered treatments for this patient will automatically appear on the Care Team list. Additionally, any consultant that was selected for a referral will be automatically added to the Care Team. This includes any lab or pharmacy that was used for a patient.

Updating the Care Team List
You manage the Care Team short list the much like other short lists. The Care Team can be accessed from the Patient Demographics screen, the Document Manager screen, the Edit Referral screen.

Details:

Uses
Use the Care Team to make choosing a consultant faster and easier. When ordering a Referral, the ordering provider knows that the consultants on the Care Team have seen the patient before.
