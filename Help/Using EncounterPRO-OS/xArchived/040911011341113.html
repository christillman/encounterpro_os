Course Description: Billing for Providers

Solution Title: Course Description: Billing for Providers
Overview:

This course is designed for the providers who will be responsible for documenting and approving encounter billing. E & M coding in EncounterPRO is covered in detail. This course is intended for all providers and may be useful for practice managers and billing specialists also.

Audience: Providers and Extenders
Prerequisites:
-- Providers

Agenda:

  • Who is Responsible for the Billing?
  • How EncounterPRO Calculates E & M Levels
  • Reviewing Encounter Billing
  • Correcting Common Billing Problems
