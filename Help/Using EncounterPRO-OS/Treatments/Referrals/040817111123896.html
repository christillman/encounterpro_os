Description
This screen is used to select the consultant or institution where you will send the patient or specimen for the lab or test ordered. The user can also indicate whether to bill for the collection and/or performance of the test.

If your practice is integrated with a laboratory, it is very important that the correct lab is selected on this screen. Otherwise, when the results are returned, they will not appear on the Daily Lab Report screen and will need to be manually posted to the correct patient and treatment.

How to Access This Screen
Access this screen by selecting the gray bar to the right of either Patient Referred to or Specimen Sent to on the lab collection screen.
