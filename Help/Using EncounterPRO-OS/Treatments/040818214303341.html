Purpose
EncounterPRO has many types of treatments available. Some of the most commonly ordered treatment types are:

  • In office drugs
  • Prescription drugs
  • Vaccinations
  • Labs and tests (both send out and in house)
  • Follow-ups
  • Referrals
Details:

Treatments in EncounterPRO are generally ordered either from the Treatment list for a specific assessment or from an Order Treatment menu. Ordering treatments from the Treatment list is preferred, as that will ensure that any billable treatments are associated with the appropriate assessment on the encounter billing.
