Purpose
If the patient was checked in to see the wrong provider, or the original provider is not available for some reason, another provider can 'take over' the encounter.

Before You Begin
The user who performs the examination service is the encounter 'owner' and will appear on the billing for the encounter.
There are security roles that determine which users can take over an encounter:

  • If the encounter is owned by a provider, only another provider can take it over
  • If the encounter is owned by a clinical staff person, either another clinical staff person or a provider can take it over.
Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. The encounter can be taken over from a few locations:
    • From the Waiting Room, select the patient name bar.
    • From the treatment room, select the patient name bar. A menu of options appears.
  2. Select the Take Over Encounter button. The system will prompt you to be sure that you want to take over the encounter.
  3. Select Yes. The patient name bar will change to the color assigned to the new provider.
