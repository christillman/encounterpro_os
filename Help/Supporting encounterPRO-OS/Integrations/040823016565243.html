Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Results from two different labs posting to same observation.

Cause: This problem is related to an incorrect association that encounterPRO has established between two different lab result sets to a single lab observation. (Example: TSH values posting to CBC's when TSH values should post to TSH lab observations only.)
Solution: Open MS Access and find the 'dbo_c_XML_Code' table. Delete all records with the 'code_domain' of 'Labcorp Catalog'.
Details:

To resolve the incorrect association of lab results to lab observations:
1) Open MS Access and find the 'dbo_c_XML_Code' table.
2) Delete all records with the 'code_domain' of 'Labcorp Catalog'.
3) During training it is not unlikely that incorrect associations could be made and therefore it is a good idea to begin with a blank slate before the 'live' production environment starts.
