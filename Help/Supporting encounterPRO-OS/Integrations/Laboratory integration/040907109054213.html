Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Practice Did Not Receive Lab Results for X Days

Cause: Not Applicable
Solution:

If a practice has not received any lab reports for any patients for several days, use this process.

Details:
  1. In CMA, go to Customer Information Page for the practice.
  2. Scroll down to the 'Messages' section and you should notice a group of results with the status 'Ready' ranging from the current date to 365 days out. If you see no results have come through CMA in the last day or more and this is the only practice missing results, the practice will need to contact the lab company for them to resend and after they do, the results should automatically transfer to the practice.

    If other practices are not receiving results, ask EPRO IT Dept. to restart EPIE Services or the dialer if practice is on the LabCorp Dialer. 
  3. Log into practice and restart their services.   Go back to CMA and the message status should, one by one start changing to 'Complete' within 2 minutes.  No resends are necessary.
