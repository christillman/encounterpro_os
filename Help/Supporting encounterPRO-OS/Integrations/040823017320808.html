Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Results posting to wrong observation

Cause: This has happened because in the past, someone at the practice manually mismapped these results.
Solution: Unmap the results by going to the lab dashboard screen. Delete the original lab treatment, reorder it for the same encounter date, and advise EHR as to which results we need to resend.
Details:

Advise the customer that he or she will need to unmap the results by going to the lab dashboard screen (for the lab to which the results were posted). See Umapping Lab Results.

Then he or she will need to delete the original lab treatment, reorder it for the same encounter date, and advise EHR as to which results we need to resend. Once the results are resent they will be waiting in scan/post to be manually posted.

Article last updated on 1/29/2008 by Adam.Zangara

Symptoms: The practice received the results, but they have posted to the wrong lab observation.
