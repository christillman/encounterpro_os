Fixing Telephone Call Encounters going to Nowhere Room

> Edit Telephone Encounter
> Determine correct workplan (write down)
> Preferences
> Workplans
> Encounter
> Search for selected workplan
> Edit workplan
> Select office
> Room assignment
> Check 1st step's room assignment, make sure the correct telephone room is selected
