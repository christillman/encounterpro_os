How to: Manage Prescription Report Printer and Paper

Solution: The prescription report has a number or attributes which can be used to manage the printer and paper used to print the prescriptions.
Details:

Attribute:          dataobject
Definition:         A string designating which powerbuilder datawindow to use
Values:             dw_prescription                        Print area width = 3.75'',  Footer = 6.354''
                        dw_prescription_size_6            Print area width = 5'',  Footer = 1.083''
                        dw_prescription_size_20          Print area width = 3.5'',  Footer = 4.864''
                        dw_prescription2                      2 signature line version
                        dw_prescription2_size_6          2 signature line version
                        dw_prescription2_size_20        2 signature line version
 
Attribute:          datawindow.print.paper.size
Definition:         An integer indicating the size of the paper used for the output:
Values:             0 - Default paper size for the printer
                        1 - Letter 8 1/2 x 11 in
                        2 - LetterSmall 8 1/2 x 11in
                        3 - Tabloid 17 x 11 inches
                        4 - Ledger 17 x 11 in
                        5 - Legal 8 1/2 x 14 in
                        6 - Statement 5 1/2 x 8 1/2 in
                        7 - Executive 7 1/4 x 10 1/2 in
                        8 - A3 297 x 420 mm
                        9 - A4 210 x 297 mm
                        10 - A4 Small 210 x 297 mm
                        11 - A5 148 x 210 mm
                        12 - B4 250 x 354 mm
                        13 - B5 182 x 257 mm
                        14 - Folio 8 1/2 x 13 in
                        15 - Quarto 215 x 275mm
                        16 - 10x14 in
                        17 - 11x17 in
                        18 - Note 8 1/2 x 11 in
                        19 - Envelope #9 3 7/8 x 8 7/8
                        20 - Envelope #10 4 1/8 x 9 ½
                        21 - Envelope #11 4 1/2 x 10 3/8
                        22 - Envelope #12 4 x 11 1/276
                        23 - Envelope #14 5 x 11 ½
                        24 - C size sheet
                        25 - D size sheet
                        26 - E size sheet
                        27 - Envelope DL 110 x 220mm

Attribute:          datawindow.print.paper.source
Definition:         An integer indicating which printer tray to pull paper from
Values:             0 - Default
                        1 - Upper
                        2 - Lower
                        3 - Middle
                        4 - Manual
                        5 - Envelope
                        6 - Envelope manual
                        7 - Auto
                        8 - Tractor
                        9 - Smallfmt
                        10 - Largefmt
                        11 - Large capacity
                        14 - Cassette

Attribute:          datawindow.footer.height
Definition:         An integer indicating how tall the footer section is in thousandth of an inch. The default footer height depends on the 'dataobject' attribute (see above). Increasing the footer height has the effect of raising the signature section on the page. Decreasing the footer height has the effect of lowering the signature section on the page.
