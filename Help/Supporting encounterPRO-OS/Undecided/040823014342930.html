How to: Disable the Reviewed time or date from the Treatment Dashboard

Solution: Right click on lab dashboard and click 'edit dashboard'. Scroll down and find 'Treatment reviewed by' and disable that command to remove the time. Alternatively highlight 'Treatment Progress' that, which states 'progress_type=Reviewed' and disable that command to remove date. Click 'ok' and click 'finish.
Details:

After a user clicks the button, the format may appear at bottom of lab dashboard as shown below:

14:50      Reviewed By Support         Results and status reviewed.
5/22/2007     Support Lab                 Results and status reviewed.

The top line lists the time lab was reviewed, which is the 'Treatment Reviewed By' command and the bottom line lists the reviewed date, which is one of the 'Treatment Progress' commands listed in the commands menu.

If practice wishes to have the time removed do the following:
1) Right click on lab dashboard and click 'edit dashboard' (with local copy active).
2) Then scroll down the commands menu to the left and find 'Treatment reviewed by' and disable that command.
3) Click 'ok' and click 'finish' and the review will now appear as follows:

5/22/2007       Support Lab        Results and status reviewed. 
 
If practice wishes to have the 'date' portion removed do the following:
1) Right click on lab dashboard and click 'edit dashboard' (with local copy active).
2) Then scroll down the commands menu to the left and find 'Treatment Progress' that, when highlighted, states 'progress_type=Reviewed' and disable that command.
3) Click 'ok' and click 'finish'. The review will now appear as follows:

09:20      Reviewed By Support Physician 5876        Test reviewed

TIP: All steps mentioned in Lab Dashboard Issues section work for any dashboards but you must perform steps for each of the dashboards. ie X-Ray dashboard, lab dashboard etc.
