How to: Sort Secondary Encounter Visit Codes

Solution:

In c_domain, filter the domain_id field for ENC_SEC_PROC .  The domain_item is not necessarily the CPT code, but is really the procedure_id from c_procedure.  Of the records with this domain_id, re-order the domain_sequence to re-order how they appear under the button on the billing screen.

To keep a secondary encounter procedure from showing up on the list, just delete its row in the c_domain table.
