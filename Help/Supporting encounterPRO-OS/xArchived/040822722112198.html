How to: Testing the Lab Interface

Solution: Log in to 'CMA' at www.jmjtech.com/mgmt, select 'Customer Management' from the links on the left, then scroll down to locate the practice and select the ID. Next, in 'Epro', check in all three lab test patients as a past date encounter.
Details:

Make sure the following three patients are entered as new patients in the client's db:
1) Lab JMJ Test, JMJ Auto
2) Lab JMJ Test, JMJ Manual
3) Lab JMJ Test, JMJ Multiple          
NOTE: If they do not appear in db then run utility script 'Enable Lab Interface' and restart services. If there is no such utility script, speak with Sumathi Asok.
 
1) Log in to 'CMA' at www.jmjtech.com/mgmt, select 'Customer Management' from the links on the left, then scroll down to locate the practice and select the ID.
2) Next, in 'Epro', check in all three lab test patients as a past date encounter. Lab Test, JMJ Manual needs to be checked in for 1/6/2006. Lab Test, JMJ Auto and Lab Test, JMJ Multiple past encounter date needs to be 3/3/2006. 
3) Order a Urine Culture (send out) for Lab Test, JMJ Manual. Make sure lab reads  before clicking 'Finish'.
4) Now select the 'Lab Company' as the consultant on the 'Collect/Refer' screen by clicking the button located next to either 'Patient Referred To:' or 'Specimen Sent To:' depending on if the practice refers the patient or collects the specimen in-house. If practice collects in-house, then click on the button to highlight it before clicking 'Finish' to send lab order.
5) In 'CMA', click 'Send Manualtest JMJ Lab Results'. *You will only click 'Send Autotest JMJ LabResults' when sending results for Lab Test, JMJ Auto.
6) Scroll down to find the 'Messages' table. You should see the message job dispatched.
7) When the 'Status' is 'Completed', (Refresh by clicking the 'Today' button) the results should be in 'Epro'. (If the message tkaes longer than 2 minutes to complete, check the o_service_schedule table and change the schedule interval time to 2 minutes.)
8) In 'Epro', go to 'Daily Lab Results' in 'Utilities'. Go to 1/6/2006. You should see the lab ordered. The 'Results' column will be blank because the mapping isn't done yet.
9) Go to 'Scan/Post' in 'Utilities' and select the .xml file for Lab Test, JMJ Manual and click 'Post'. In the next screen select the Urine Culture lab dated 1/6/2006 located to the left under 'Existing Treatments' section then click 'Post to Treatment'.
10) Return to Daily Lab Results, 1/6/2006. You should see today's date and 'Final' in the 'Results' column.
11) Repeat steps 3 thru 7 for Lab Test, JMJ Auto. *For step 5 click 'Send Autotest JMJ LabResults' only. When the message is complete, the 'Daily Lab Results' screen for 3/3/2006 should show the Results received today. Because the mapping for Urine Culture was already done in step 9, you do not have to do it again for this lab.
12) Click on the 'Review' button for both labs. You can review from 'Daily Lab Results', 'To Do List', or 'Outstanding Treatment/Tests'.
13) Now order a Urine Culture for 3/3/2006 for Lab Test, JMJ Multiple using steps 3 and 4 only.   
14) Go to 'Scan/Post' in 'Utilities'. Select the xml file for Lab Test, JMJ Multiple. Since the CBC was not pre-ordered, it will not appear in the 'Existing Treatments' list on the left.
15) Create a new treatment (menu located on the right)  by selecting  'Treatment type' and clicking 'Lab'.
16) Select lab named 'CBC w/DIFF w/PLT', confirm the begin date of 3/3/2006 then select 'New Treatment'. *The CBC will NOT appear on Daily Lab Results nor will it send a TODO item but the results have posted to the chart.
17) In 'Epro', go to 'Daily Lab Results' for 3/3/2006. The Urine Culture for Multiple should have auto-posted. The results for the CBC have not.
