How to: Instruct the encounterPRO Users in the Lab Integration

Solution: Training documentation: This is the training document that gets sent to each new lab interface user. This document outlines the process from beginning to end (this document is saved on the JMJ network): \\Labserv2003\common\Training\Epro 40 materials and documentation\Current documents\lab_integration (2).doc
Trainer Checklist: This corresponding KB article serves as a check list for trainers prior to training a practice on the lab interface. Regardless of whether or not the automatic script has been run, trainers should still check this: https://www.jmjtech.com/KB/Article.aspx?ArticleNumber=10641
Details:

Prior to Scheduling Training with the Practice
1) Confirm that the 'Enable Addressee' box is checked in CMA. (See section VI) 
2) Set up the default workplan for the Lab treatment type. (See section I)
3) Confirm the three Lab Test patients are in the practice's database. (See section VII) 
4) Confirm that a 'Daily Lab Results' button appears in 'Utilities'. (See section II)
5) Go through the steps to order labs and send messages from the CMA to the practice to confirm that the messages are received by the practice and perform the manual posting; repeat to check the auto-posting. (See Section III) 
6) Create a 'Reviewed' button on the Lab Dashboard if necessary. (See Section VII )
7) Remove the mapping you created in the previous steps. (See Section V )
8) Cancel the labs you created in the steps above. (See section V)
9) Schedule the training with the practice.

Day Before the Training Session
1) Confirm training date and time with practice.
2) Send 'EncounterPro Laboratory Integration' training booklet to practice via email or fax.
3) Send a message to all users in the Epro client instructing them to continue to post/scan normally but to be aware not to delete any xml files from in the post/scan utility that JMJ has imported for training purposes.

During the Training Session
You will conduct a remote training session and perform steps in section III and in 'EncounterPro Laboratory Integration' training booklet, to demonstrate to practice the below steps for a lab interface:
1) How to order labs and select the consultant.
2) Show the 'Daily Lab Referral' list.
3) Go to 'Scan/Post' and show how to manually post the results.
4) Have the client order a 'Urine culture Send Out Int' for Lab Test, JMJ Auto. *Other labs wont work during training. 
5) Go to 'Daily Lab Results' and point out that the 'Urine Culture' auto-posted due to the mapping you performed with Lab Test, JMJ Manual.
6) In 'Scan/Post', locate the 'Urine Culture Send Out' .xml file for Lab Test, JMJ Multiple and show client how to post a treatment that was not previously ordered. (New treatment menu).
7) Ask client if there are additional labs that they require to be configured.
8) Ask the customer who (user or role) should receive the ToDo to review lab results. (See section I)
9) Ask customer which screens/menus they would like to have default to the lab company's information if any. (See Section I)
