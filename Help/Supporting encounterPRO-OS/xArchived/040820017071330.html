How to: Set Decimal Numbers to Display a Leading Zero

Solution: Results and dosages less than 1 print without a leading zero (ex. .3 instead of 0.3)
Details:

In c_unit, in the column display_mask, for all the values that look like #.# or #.## etc, replace the first # with 0 (0.#, 0.##)
