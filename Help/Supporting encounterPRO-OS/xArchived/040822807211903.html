How to: Unmap and Cancel Labs

Solution: Unmapping and canceling labs involves three steps. First, remove the mapping of a lab. Next, remove the mapping of two labs. Finally, cancel an ordered lab.
Details:

Remove the mapping of a lab
1) While in the lab dashboard of the lab you have ordered click 'Other Items', select the black X button titled 'UNDO Lab Map' and follow any further steps that may pop up.
2) Sometimes an 'EPRO' pop box will appear after clicking this button where you have to select the lab in a drop down type box. Simply follow those instructions.
3) The reason for this is to remove the mapping created during testing so that during training the practice may see how a lab is mapped and eventually auto posts. When completed a white screen should appear and the  should appear.
 NOTE: If un-mapping does not work or if ' ' appears on the white unmap screen, run Un-Do Lab Mapping Utility Script and then HotFix 192. (See also kb article #10289 'Lab Results Posted To Wrong Lab')
 
Remove the mapping of two labs
If a user maps two labs incorrectly, for example a user posts 'Urine Culture' results to a 'Thin Prep' they must unmap each lab involved by doing the above steps but for each lab involved.

Canceling an ordered lab
While in the lab dashboard of the lab you have ordered click 'Other Items', click the trash can button titled 'cancel lab' and follow next few steps that pop up.
