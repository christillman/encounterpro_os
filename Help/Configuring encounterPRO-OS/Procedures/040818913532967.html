Description
Procedures are the items and activities that you can bill for within encounterPRO. encounterPRO uses the current CPT codes for it's procedures.

Types of Procedures
The types of procedures you can configure in encounterPRO are:

  • Primary Encounter
  • Secondary Encounter
  • Procedures
  • Drug/Item Administration
  • HCPCS
  • Perform Procedures
  • Collect Procedures
  • Vaccine Administration

Procedures are configured and maintained much like assessments. In fact, the screens look very similar.
