How to: Create a New Procedure Category

Solution: Add a new category to c_procedure_category by choosing a procedure_type from c_procedure_type, creating a procedure_category_id and entering a description.  Then add a new row to c_specialty_procedure_category with the practice's specialty_id, and the procedure_type and procedure_category that you just created in the other table.  You don't need a value in the sort_sequence field.
