Definition
The Reports Library is a repository of EncounterPRO reports. The Library contains reports that have been developed and tested by EHR as well as custom reports created and shared by EncounterPRO users. Reports can be imported from the Reports Library at any time, allowing your practice continuous access to the latest reports available. The list is refreshed each night during the nightly maintenance job and can also be refreshed manually by clicking on the Refresh button. EncounterPRO users who have created custom reports and wish to share those reports with other EncounterPRO users can export or upload reports to the Reports Library. These reports will be labeled in the Reports Library as 'Shareware.'

CAUTION: Shareware reports are not warranted or maintained by EHR Product Support. If there is a defect in a shareware report, there will be a fee charged if you wish EHR Product Support to troubleshoot or repair the report.
