Triage Protocol: Billing is Not Coming Across to Practice Management System

Triage Protocol: Triage Protocol: Billing is Not Coming Across to Practice Management System
Overview:

The PMS system is not receiving billing information from EncounterPRO.

The most common cause of billing information not crossing over into the PMS is that the EncounterPRO Services have stopped.  

Symptoms:

Get Patient Examples

q  Billing for a specific patient is not showing up in the PMS (get patient name, id number, and encounter date)

q  Billing for a group of patient is not showing up in the PMS (get patient names, id numbers, and encounter dates). What does this group of patients have in common? Same visit type, same provider, all are new patients, etc?

q  No billing information is showing up in the PMS (get representative patient name, id number, and encounter date)

Determine when the problem started

q  Today

q  Yesterday

q  More than two days ago

Determine billing icon

Ask the caller to go to the Checkout screen, locate a patient with the billing problem and describe the billing icon to the right of the patient name bar.

If the billing icon is a?

Then?

  Red checkmark

The billing was not sent to the PMS.

   Red $ with an X through it

The billing was sent to the PMS, but was rejected for some reason.

  Red $

The encounter is not billable. Is this supposed to be a billable encounter? Check the encounter type.

 Envelope

The Envelope means that the procedure failed.

  Green checkmark

The billing information is being sent from EncounterPRO, but something is preventing the information from appearing in your PMS.

Resolution:
Agent connects to site using non-Admin login. Stop and re-start EncounterPRO Services.

After waiting a minute or so, ask the caller to go back to the Checkout screen and locate a patient that was having the billing problem. Ask them to describe the billing icon to the right of the patient name bar.

If the billing icon is a?

Then?

 Red checkmark

The billing was not sent to the PMS.

If the billing has not been coming across for several days, it will take a few minutes for the system to 'catch up'.  Wait a few minutes and re-check; go back a few days on the Checkout screen to when the billing stopped working and see if those icons have changed to Green checkmarks or Dollar bills (success).

If no change after several minutes, Agent records the information and all steps performed in  the ticket and assigns to Level 2.

  Red $ with an X through it

The billing was sent to the PMS, but was rejected for some reason.

Agent records the information and all steps performed in  the ticket and assigns to Level 2.

  Red $

The encounter is not billable. Is this supposed to be a billable encounter? Check the encounter type.

 Envelope

The Envelope means that the procedure failed.

Agent records the information and all steps performed in  the ticket and assigns to Level 2.

  Green checkmark

The billing information is being sent from EncounterPRO, but something is preventing the information from appearing in the PMS.

Agent verifies that the billing messages are in the PMShare folder.  Update this information in the ticket and assign to??.

EncounterPRO is sending the information, but it is not being picked up by the PMS, the caller will need to contact his/her PMS Support to resolve this problem.
