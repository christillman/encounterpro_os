Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Reviewer name not appearing

Cause: Not Applicable
Solution: Right click inside a lab dashboard, click 'Edit Display Script'. Scroll for 'Treatment Reviewed By' or 'Treatment Progress', when highlighted, states 'progress_type=Reviewed' and enable one or both of those commands. Click 'ok' and click 'finish'.
Details:

To allow the dashboard to show which user reviewed the lab perform the following:
1) Right click anywhere inside a lab dashboard, create a local copy if necessary then, click 'Edit Display Script' in the commands menu to the left.
2) Scroll for the 'Treatment Reviewed By' or 'Treatment Progress' that, when highlighted, states 'progress_type=Reviewed' and enable one or both of those commands.
3) Click 'ok' and click 'finish'. (See Disabling the time or date command from appearing after clicking 'reviewed' to understand differences of the two commands.)
TIP: All steps mentioned in Lab Dashboard Issues section work for any dashboards but you must perform steps for each of the dashboards. ie X-Ray dashboard, lab dashboard etc.
