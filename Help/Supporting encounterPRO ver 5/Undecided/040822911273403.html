Scanning Guidelines - black and white vs. grayscale

Solution: We recommend scanning in B\W in 150 dpi vs Grayscale. A simple text page document when scanned using HP Precision Scan PRO in B\W - 259 KB and in Grayscale - 2,060.
Details:

1) We highly recommend scanning in B\W in 150 dpi vs Grayscale. See below comparisons.
2) A simple text page document when scanned using HP Precision Scan PRO
  a) B\W - 259 KB
  b) Grayscale - 2,060
3) When an image is scanned as a B\W image into EncounterPRO we then convert that file into a tif file.
