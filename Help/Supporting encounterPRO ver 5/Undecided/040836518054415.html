Resolving discrepancies between Vaccine View Details and Immunization Maker report

Cause: An encounter was cancelled and then re-opened, which seems to have set the vaccine to having a status of Open, a value for who closed the treatment, and no end date.  The View Details for vaccines lists all non-cancelled vaccines, while the Immunization Maker report only lists vaccines that are Closed.
Solution: Click on the vaccine from the SOAP screen to get to the dashboard and close the treatment.
Details: This was for a version 4 site, and hopefully the un-cancel function has been improved.
Symptoms: Vaccine details and Immunization Maker show different treatments
