Overview:

Caller reports that the practice is having trouble logging in to encounterPRO.

Symptoms:

q  The caller cannot log in to EncounterPRO

q  Some users cannot log in to EncounterPRO

q  No users can log in to EncounterPRO

 

Resolution:

q  The caller cannot log in to EncounterPRO from a specific workstation (??)

Then?

Can log in using the caller's credentials
