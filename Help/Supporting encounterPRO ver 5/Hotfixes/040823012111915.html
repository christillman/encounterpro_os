Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Results not appearing in encounterPRO

Cause: Not Applicable
Solution: The results do not show up in Epro, or take a very long time. First check the o_service_schedule table. If the setting is 2 min, then check to make sure that background services are running on the practice's server. Lab results not appearing in the 'To Be Posted' list or 'Daily Lab Results' list, even though CMA says that results were sent. Results may have been posted to a Canceled lab. You must un-map the mapping for that particular type of lab (ex, Urine Culture). Re-send results from CMA. Then the results will show up in the 'To Be Posted' List. The practice did not receive the results. Sometimes this is just a matter of restarting the services, especially if the status of the results in CMA is 'Ready'. If that doesn't work, select the results in CMA that need to be resent and resend them. If the practice did not receive some results and you cannot find these specific results in CMA, then advise the customer to call the lab company and have them resend those results because we have not received them. Or, if you know into which folder the lab company is sending the results (either to our server or the customer's) you can check that folder to see if we received the results but they produced errors.
Details: Confirm these settings if you are having problems:
1) Check the workplan and folder configuration settings. They should appear as below:
dbo_c_workplan_item_attribute

2192 8134   wait_progress_key status

NOTE: Some practices may have different folder name for their labs. The folder name can be located in dbo_c_folder table. ie 'lab reports' or 'lab data' folder.

2) Confirm EPRO is setup to accept messages from EPIE:
  a) Select 'Config' and click 'external sources'.
  b) Pick 'JMJ lab results' and 'File import'.
  c) Click 'Finished'.
3) If 'Post To Existing Treatment' is not appearing or is blank when trying to post labs in 'scan/post' and labs have been pre-ordered, confirm the setting for the treatment workplan is set for 'Lab tests'.
