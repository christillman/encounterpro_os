How to: Remove the sick/well waiting room

Solution:

If the practice only has one waiting room and you want to remove the menu that asks which waiting room (Sick or Well) you want to put the patient into, go to the database. Open the 'o_rooms' table. Change status of 'OK' to 'NA'. Change the Description of the other waiting room from 'Sick/Well Waiting Room' to 'Waiting Room'. Close the table.

Details:

If the practice only has one waiting room and you want to remove the menu that appears when you manually check-in a patient that asks which waiting room (Sick or Well) you want to put the patient into:

Resolution

1) Go to the database (cpr or whatever their db name is).
2) Open the 'o_rooms' table.
3) Change one of the waiting rooms from status of 'OK' to 'NA'.
4) Change the Description of the other waiting room from 'Sick/Well Waiting Room' to simply 'Waiting Room'. Close the table.

The change should be in effect immediately. You should not have to re-start Epro.
