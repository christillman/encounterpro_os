Troubleshooting Lab Integration: Incorrect sender displayed for Task

Cause: Not Applicable
Solution: Right click on lab dashboard and click 'edit dashboard'. Scroll down and find 'Treatment services' and highlight it. Click inside 'Command Attributes' box and change the service field to 'EPRO Todo Item'. Click 'next' four times then 'finish. Click 'ok' and 'finish'.
Details:

After sending an 'InBox ToDo' from a lab dashboard, the message may appear as follows near the bottom of dashboard:

From: Support Physician 9876 To: Support Physician 9876 Dispatched: 5/22/2007 16:08:40 Status: Completed 5/22/2007 16:09:22

Subject: Send Todo Item 
*Notice it reads from 'support' to 'support'.

To fix:
1) Right click on lab dashboard and click 'edit dashboard' (with local copy active).
2) Then scroll down the command menu to the left and find 'Treatment services' (should be first one listed, there may be two) and highlight it.
3) Then left click inside the 'Command Attributes' box and change the service field to 'EPRO Todo Item'.
4) Click 'next' four times then finally 'finish.
5) Click 'ok' and then 'finish' again. It will automatically change all messages to appear as follows:

From: Support Physician 9876 To: Amber G. Moore, R.N. Dispatched: 5/22/2007 16:09:21 Status: Dispatched

TIP: All steps mentioned in Lab Dashboard Issues section work for any dashboards but you must perform steps for each of the dashboards. ie X-Ray dashboard, lab dashboard etc.
