Description
This screen contains a great deal of information about a patient, such as:

  • General demographic information such as address, birthdate, etc. If you have an integrated billing and scheduling system, you will update this information in that system, not in encounterPRO. encounterPRO reads the patient information from the billing and scheduling system and any information you change in encounterPRO will be over-written by the information in the billing and scheduling system.
  • Patient aliases, in the case of legal name changes
  • Email, telephone and other communication information
  • Insurance carriers
  • Current and previous encounters
  • Related patients within your practice

How to Access This Screen
This screen can be accessed by:

  • Selecting a patient name bar on the Charts tab (after searching for a patient)
  • Selecting the Edit Patient Data button on the Patient Chart (may need to select Other Items first)
  • Selecting the Edit Patient Data button on the menu from the Treatment Room, Waiting Room, Checkout Room, etc.
