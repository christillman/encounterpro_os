Purpose
Some practices have more than one office location. When encounterPRO users work in more than one office location, it is important that they log in to the office location where they are actively working.

The Office Location affects the rooms in the Office View and can affect other behaviors of encounterPRO that are set on a location by location basis. For more information, see Concept: Multiple Office Locations.

Before You Begin
The office location you are currently logged into appears at the top of the Office View in the dark blue bar.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to encounterPRO.
  2. From the Office View, select the Utilities tab. The Utilities list appears.
  3. Select Change Office. The Change Office screen appears with a list of office locations for your practice.
  4. Select the office location you are currently working in (it will appear darkened).
  5. If you want this location to be the location that you are automatically logged into, set it as the default.
  6. Select Finished. The Utilities list reappears.
