How to: Interrupt and Resume an Encounter

Purpose
It is quite common that during an encounter a user is interrupted and needs to put the encounter 'on hold' for a while. Most encounterPRO screens give the user the option of selecting I'll Be Back.

Before You Begin
When you select I'll Be Back, the system creates a service bar in the Office View so that you can easily resume the task you were performing. If the original user is unable to complete the task another user can 'take over' that service bar and complete the task.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. Do one of the following:
    • From the Vitals, Allergies, History screens select I'll Be Back.
    • From the Patient Chart select Exit, then select I'll Be Back The system may prompt you to either leave the task assigned to you or leave it unassigned for the role the task was originally ordered for.

    The service bar for the task appears in the Office View.

  2. To resume working on a task, select the service bar. The screen you were previously working on reappears.
  3. To take over a task that was assigned to another individual user (not simply assigned to the role), select the service bar. The system prompts you to indicate whether you want to take over the task. Select Yes. Note: Only providers can take over the Examination service from another provider. The user who 'owns' the Examination service is the Encounter Owner for billing purposes.
