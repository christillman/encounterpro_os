Solution Title: Course Description: Introduction to encounterPRO version 5
Overview:

This course is designed to give participants an overview of encounterPRO version 5 and will cover several important concepts and basic functions. This course is required by all encounterPRO version 5 users.

Audience: All users
Prerequisites: None

Agenda:

  • Logging on and off
  • A tour of the Office View
  • Looking up Patient Charts
  • What is Workflow?
