Description
In version 5 a new concept is introduced: Documents. Documents are used with the Document Manager in order to output information to various formats. Documents are very similar to the traditional encounterPR reports. The following table shows the difference between Documents and Reports.

Documents

Reports

Are dispatched using Document Manager to recipients and routes. Routes can include:

    • Printer
    • Secure email *
    • Secure Fax *
    • e-Prescribing

* Requires a 3rd party application

Are sent to printers and/or faxed

Are automatically saved to the patient's chart as attachments

Are not automatically saved

Are available at three levels:

    • Treatment
    • Encounter
    • Patient

 

Are available at four levels:

    • Treatment
    • Encounter
    • Patient
    • General
