Description
The Review of Systems (ROS) screen is used to document symptoms or observations by body system. In many cases, this screen is also used to document any additional complaints that were not documented in the Chief Complaint or History of Present Illness (HPI). The ROS counts towards the history portion of the E & M Coding for an office visit.
ROS screens can be configured as either drilldown or in the Yes/No format.

How to Access This Screen
Access this screen by:

  • Selecting the small icon to the left of Review of Systems (ROS) on the Patient Chart
  • Selecting the description of Review of Systems (ROS) on the Patient Chart, then selecting the description again on the dashboard
  • Selecting the ROS button on the Add History menu from the Patient Chart
