Purpose
During well child visits, the provider or clinical staff can decide to document the anticipatory guidelines for that age group with the parents.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Patient Chart select the Pick H and P button. A menu of histories appears. Then select the Child Health Ed button. This will display the Anticipatory Guidance for the age of your patient. If your practice has the Anticipatory Guidance pre-ordered, select the icon to the left of the blue description, Anticipatory Guidance.
  2. Select Anticipatory Guidance. The Anticipatory Guidance screen for the patient's age group displays.
  3. Document the safety and health topics discussed.
  4. Select Finished. The Patient Chart reappears.
