How to: Document Child Vitals in Pounds and Ounces.

Purpose
The Child Vitals screen has been configured to accept weights in pounds and ounces as well as in decimal format.

Before You Begin
You need to know if your scale measures in pounds and ounces or decimal places.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Child Vitals screen, do one of the following:
    • Select the Weight field and type the digits for pounds, then press the spacebar on your keyboard and enter the digits for ounces
    • Select the Ellipsis button (...) to the right of the Weight field. Select the digits for pounds, select the decimal point on the number pad then select the digits for ounces. Select OK.
    • Select the Weight field and type the digits for pounds and tenths (decimal form)
  2. When you tab out of the field or select a new field the weight will be translated to display as pounds and ounces.
