Description
This screen allows clinical staff and providers to review the immunization rules for a specific vaccine, as defined on the Recommended Childhood and Adolescent Immunization Schedule U.S. 2006.
The ProVacs uses these rules to determine when a patient is due, past due, or ineligible for a particular dose of a vaccine.


NOTE: Only medical personnel can decide when a patient should receive a particular vaccine. The encounterPRO ProVacs is meant only to assist the provider's role in determining patient care.

How to Access this Screen
Access this screen by:

  1. Selecting the ProVacs tab on the Patient Chart.
  2. Selecting the Give Now/Future Date/Completed/Ineligible button for a specific vaccine.
  3. Selecting Show Rules on the Vaccine Schedule screen.
