How to: Document Past Immunizations.

Purpose
On all well visits for Family Practice and Pediatric specialties the clinical staff will have the option of recording the patient's past vaccines. When your practice begins using encounterPRO you may want the staff to enter past vaccines for each patient. Only by entering past vaccines will the system be able to produce the encounterPRO vaccination reports and ProVacs screen accurately.
Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Patient Chart, select the Vaccines tab. The Immunization Status screen appears.
  2. Select View Detail. The Immunization Record screen appears.
  3. Select New. The New Past Immunization screen appears.
  4. Select the appropriate vaccine(s) on the left side of the screen, and then fill in the appropriate boxes on the right side of the screen. The Date the shot was given is required.
  5. Select OK. The Immunization Record screen reappears. The newly entered immunizations are highlighted in green.
  6. If you want to enter additional information for a specific vaccine (such as lot number), select that cell in the table and enter the information. This action is available only while the row for that vaccine is highlighted in green. It will remain highlighted for the remainder of the calendar day.
  7. Repeat steps 3 - 6 for each past immunization.
  8. When all past immunizations have been entered, select Finished to return to the Patient Chart. The Immunization Status screen should update to reflect the immunizations entered.
