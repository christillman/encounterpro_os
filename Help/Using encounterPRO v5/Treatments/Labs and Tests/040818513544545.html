Purpose
When a lab or test requiring a specimen is ordered, a service bar will appear for the Clinical Staff in the Office View.

Before You Begin
What do you need to know before starting?

Details:

Steps
Follow these steps:

  1. If the lab or test ordered has a treatment mode that requires a collect service, a service bar appears in the Office View.
  2. Select the Collect Specimen service bar. The collect screen appears for the treatment. Collect screens will differ from treatment to treatment.
  3. Do one of the following:
    • If the specimen was collected in the office, select Collected.
    • If the specimen will be sent to an outside laboratory for processing, select Specimen Sent To then select the laboratory that will process the specimen. If your practice is integrated with a lab, such as Quest or Labcorps, be sure to select that as the consultant. Otherwise, the lab results may require manual posting.
    • If the patient was sent to an outside provider, select Patient Sent To, then select the laboratory that the patient will go to for the specimen collection.
  4. Select Finished. If this lab is being processed in the office, an Enter Results service bar appears in the Office View.
