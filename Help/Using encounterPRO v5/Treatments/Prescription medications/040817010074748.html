Description
This screen is used to alert the provider that the treatment he or she is attempting to order is contraindicated, usually due to a patient's allergies.

The text area of the screen displays the reason that the treatment in question triggered the contraindication alert.

How to Access This Screen

This screen can be accessed by selecting the red 'A' that appears to the right of a contraindicated treatment on any treatment list. (If no treatments are contraindicated, the 'A' will not appear.)
