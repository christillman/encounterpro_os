How to: Recheck a Patient into the Office.

Purpose
If the provider or one of the clinical staff selects I'm Finished on the Billing Review screen by mistake and sends the patient to the Checkout Room prematurely, he or she can re-open the encounter to continue charting.

Before You Begin
If the billing has already been sent to the integrated PMS before you re-check the patient, you will need to manually correct the billing information in the billing system. Be sure to send a Task to your billing specialist.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. Select the Checkout tab. A list of patients who have had encounters today appears.
  2. Select the name bar of the patient for whom you want to re-open the encounter. A menu of options appears.
  3. Select the Recheck button. You will be asked to verify that you want to re-open a closed encounter.
  4. Select Yes, and then select the room where the patient is located.
