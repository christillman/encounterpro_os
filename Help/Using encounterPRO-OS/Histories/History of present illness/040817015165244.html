Description
This screen is used to document additional details about the reason for today's visit that were not documented in the Chief Complaint. There are a number of different HPI screens in EncounterPRO with different formats (Yes/No vs. Drilldown). Additionally, there are HPI screens configured with observations for specific conditions, such as ADHD or Diabetes.


NOTE: A minimal level of E & M coding credit is given towards the HPI when the Chief Complaint is documented. To reach an extended or complete level for the HPI, a separate HPI screen must be completed.
