Course Description: Front Desk Administration

Solution Title: Course Description: Front Desk Administration
Overview:

 

This course is designed for the Front Desk Administrators and those users who will perform functions such as checking in patients and scheduling follow-ups.

Audience: Front desk personnel, Referral Coordinator
Prerequisites:
-- Introduction to encounterPRO-OS
-- encounterPRO Basics

Agenda:

  • EncounterPRO Tasks Typical for Role
  • Checking in Patients
  • Maintaining Patient Demographic Information
  • Exporting Patient Charts
  • Exiting a Patient from the Office (Close Encounter)
  • Scheduling Follow-up Visits
  • Printing Reports and Documents
