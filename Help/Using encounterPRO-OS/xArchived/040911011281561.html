Course Description: Clinical Staff

Solution Title: Course Description: Clinical Staff
Overview:

This course is designed for the clinical staff at the practice. It will cover the functions typically performed by that role. There are several practice assignments to perform using encounterPRO to help each user become familiar with the basic clinical staff functions.

Audience: Clinical Staff/Nursing personnel
Prerequisites:
-- Introduction to encounterPRO-OS
-- encounterPRO Basics

Agenda:

  • Clinical Staff Workflow
  • Overview of the Patient Chart
  • Completing Services Ordered by the Provider
  • Managing Labs and Tests
  • Ordering Treatments
  • Documenting Nurse-only Encounters
  • Charting Past Encounters
  • Moving Patients Around in the Office
  • Documenting Immunizations
