Course Description: Billing Specialist

Solution Title: Course Description: Billing Specialist
Overview:

This course covers how to maintain the library of CPT and ICD-9 codes in EncounterPRO. It also covers how to review and correct encounter billing and how to review billing for the entire practice. This course is intended for the Billing Specialists in your practice; however, the Practice Manager would also find this information useful.

Agenda:

  • Tasks Typical for the Billing Specialist
  • Identifying the Appropriate Billing Codes
  • Maintaining the Database of Codes
  • Correcting Encounter Billing
  • Reviewing Daily Billing
  • Reviewing Practice Billing
