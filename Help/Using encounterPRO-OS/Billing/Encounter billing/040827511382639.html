Description

The Coding Rules/Encounter Data screen allows a user to review in detail what items were counted towards the E & M encounter level for a particular encounter. This screen is available from the E & M Coding Levels screen and can be displayed for each Item/Type and Level Achieved.

Us this screen in conjunction with the E & M Coding Rules screen when trying to determine why an encounter is coding too high or too low. The E & M Coding Rules screen will display the rule used to calcaluate the level for one of the three categories: History, Examination, and Decision Making. If that information is not specific enough, this screen can be displayed for individual items/types within the category to see specifics on what was counted.

For the item/type chosen, this screen displays the E & M Category and E & M Element for the specific observations documented. This can tell the user one of two things:

  • If an observation was documented in the encounter and does not appear on the screen, this can indicate that the observation was not configured to give proper E & M credit.
  • If an observation was not documented during the encounter (such as the HPI or ROS) the user can go back to the chart and complete that documentation.

IMPORTANT: EncounterPRO calculates a suggested visit level based on the amount of information that was documented during the encounter. The system can only measure the work done, it cannot evaluate the necessity of the work. Ultimately it is the encounter owner or provider, not encounterPRO who is responsible for the E & M levels chosen. The system merely offers a suggested level based on the observations recorded, and gives the provider the option to view the reasons that this level is suggested, but it is he or she who chooses the encounter level that is approved and sent to billing

How to Access This Screen
Access this screen by:

  1. Selecting the number button for E & M Coding Level on the Patient Chart or Exit tab.
  2. Selecting the Details button below the category for which you want to view more information (History, Examination, Decision Making).
  3. Selecting the bar for Level Achieved on the Coding Rules screen for the Item/Type you want to review. The Coding Rules tab appears first. To view the items that were counted for this item/type in the encounter, select the Encounter Data tab.
