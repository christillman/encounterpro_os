Description
In general, assessments are the diagnoses the provider determines appropriate given a critical analysis and evaluation of the patient's condition. encounterPRO uses the ICD-9 diagnosis codes.
encounterPRO is delivered with a large database of common assessments (diagnoses). encounterPRO categorizes assessments into the following types:
  • Allergy
  • OB
  • Sick
  • Telephone (used for telephone calls)
  • Vaccine ('V' codes)
  • Well

NOTE: The type of assessments you see will vary depending of the specialty of your practice.

Auto Close Rules
Individual assessments can be configured to automatically close after a specified period of time. The delivered database will set the auto close rule to 180 days for Well assessments and most acute Sick assessments. Chronic sick assessments will not have an auto close rule and will remain open until manually closed.
