How to: Clean a Dirty Treatment Room

Purpose
When the office staff has completed all tasks for a visit, the system automatically sends the patient to the Checkout screen. The room where the patient was located will then show as 'Dirty'. This feature lets the staff know that the room needs to be cleaned before assigning another patient to that room.

TIP: There is a General Preference that controls whether the Dirty flag appears for Treatment Rooms. If your practice would prefer not to see 'Dirty'  or 'Empty' on the Office View, the Super User can change that Preference globally.

Details:

Steps
When the actual exam room has been cleaned:

  1. Select that room from the Office View.
  2. Select the Cleaned button to let the system know that the room has been cleaned. The room will show as Empty on the Office View.
