How to: Use the Solution Center

Solution:

There are several 'boxes' on the Solution Center home page to allow you to perform tasks and get information. These boxes are called portlets. Currently the portlets available are:

Announcements

Announcements are managed by the Solution Center administrator at EHR. Typically the information provided relates to new products, upgrades, help desk broadcasts, or other 'for your information' material.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions are managed by the Solution Center administrator at EHR. To use the FAQs:

  1. Select the frequently asked question solution link. (The question and answers are displayed.)
  2. At the bottom of the solution, you have several options for using the solution:
    • Select the 'Yes' button if the solution solved your question or problem.
    • Select the 'No' continue to search button if the solution did not solve your problem and to search for another answer.
    • Select the 'No' open a ticket button to open a help desk ticket for assistance from your IT support team.
    • From a solution, you can also print the solution by selecting the 'Printer Friendly' button. (The solution will open in another window in a printable format.)
      1. Click the 'Print' button to send the solution to your printer.
      2. Click 'Print' on the printer dialog window.
Search

    Being able to quickly and easily locate useful information is one of the main focuses of the Solution Center. There are three ways to search within the Solution Center:

    • Standard text search
    • Browsing by category or
    • A combination of both

    Click here to view Show-IT.  This will display a Flash movie tutorial on how to Search in the Solution Center.

    To search using a standard text search:

    1. Enter your search in the Search field. Some tips for searching:

    • A search string should contain a minimum of three unique words.
    • Unique words do not need to include such words as: the, not, a, and, at .
    • When searching for on error-related issues use the complete error message.
    • The portal allows for more advanced searching by using certain search operators. These operators include: the plus sign (+), and minus sign (-).
    • Use a plus sign to force a word to be present, or the minus sign to force a word not to be present.
    • Finally, use the 'Search Within Results' option when narrowing a search.

    To browse by category:

    1. Select the 'Find Answers' tab to reveal all the available categories, also commonly referred to as taxonomy.
    2. Drilldown through the categories listed under Browse Results by Category to find the desired category. A list of solutions that belong to the selected category appears in the Results box.
    3. Select an underlined title from the list to view that solution.

    To combine these two methods:

    1. First select the 'Find Answers' Tab.
    2. Navigate to the desired category under Browse Results By Category. A list of solutions that belong to the selected category appears in the Results box.
    3. Enter a search string in the search field then select Search. This will only search within the list of solutions in the category displayed.
    4. To perform a new search select the 'Start over' link.

    Upcoming Events

    The Upcoming Events portlet displays educational opportunities and other encounterPRO events for the next 4-6 weeks. A link to the full encounterPRO University webinar calendar is provided.

    Did You Know?

    The Did You Know portlet displays productivity tip solutions based on the search terms entered. The solutions displayed in the Did You Know portlet relate to the solutions in the search results area that may be helpful in other similar tasks you are trying to perform.

    To view and use Did You Knows:

    1. Execute a search (The Did You Know portlet will be displayed on the right hand side).
    2. Select the solution title to review the complete solution.
    3. Select the 'More' link to see a complete list of related tips.
    4. If you find the solution helpful, you may want to add the solution to 'My Favorites' by clicking the 'Add to favorites' button.

    My Favorites

     The 'My Favorite' solutions portlet is designed to allow you to save solutions for quick and easy access to those solutions you most commonly need to view.

    To add solution to 'My Favorites':
    1. Search or browse to locate a solution.
    2. View the solution you wish to mark as a favorite.
    3. Select the 'Add to favorites' button at the bottom of the solution.

    To manage the solutions in 'My Favorites'.
    1. Click on the 'Edit My Favorite's' link.
    2. Select the checkbox of the solution you wish to delete. 
        a) You can select more than one solution or click the 'Select All' button to select all the solutions.

    3. Select the 'Delete Selected Solutions from Favorites' button. (The selection solutions are deleted from My Favorites.)
    NOTE: Some solutions in 'My Favorites'' may be listed by the Solution Center administrator and cannot be removed.
