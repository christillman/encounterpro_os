How to: Close/Complete a Task

  • Want to Learn More?

Purpose
When someone assigns you a Task or you take ownership of a task assigned to one of your roles you need to complete the assigned action, then close the Task. When you close the Task, the item moves to your Completed list. If you cannot complete the Task, you can forward it to another user or role. See How to: Forward Tasks for instructions.
Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Office view, select the Tasks tab.
  2. Locate the Task item you want to complete on the My Tasks list or on one of the lists for your roles.
  3. Select the gray bar of the Task. The screen appears to allow you to complete the associated task.
  4. At this point, you can select one of the following:
    • I'll Be Back - takes ownership of the item, places the item on your My Tasks list, and removes it from the role Task list.
    • I'm Finished - completes and closes the Task and places the item on the My Tasks list in the completed folder
