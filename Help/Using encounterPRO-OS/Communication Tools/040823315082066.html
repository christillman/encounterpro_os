Solution Path: Using encounterPRO-OS > Communication Tools

How to: Contact EHR Product Support.

Solution: This solution gives some information on how to work most effectively with the EHR Product Support Team.
Details:

Help Us Clearly Define the Issue

Whether or not the issue or request is related to a single chart, user, workstation, location, or group helps in defining the problem.  Similarly, whether or not the issue is consistently repeatable or possibly linked to another activity helps.

Is the Issue New or Old? 

If others in your office have experienced and resolved this same issue in the past, knowing how it was resolved might prevent you from even needing to contact someone at EHR.  If it is a previously reported but unresolved issue, please reference the issue number when calling.

Provide Examples

Please be ready to provide examples or recreate the issue.  If a request for an enhancement is being made, please help us understand the context of why a particular change is desired.

Contact Us

The best way to contact us to insure proper communication of the issue is through email at Encounterprosupport@encounterpro.com.  You can also call us anytime between 7:00AM and 7:00PM Eastern Monday through Friday for assistance at 877-919-2208.  Urgent after-hours inquiries can be made by following the instructions after dialing 877-919-2208.

You can also start a new support ticket by selecting the No, Create a Help Ticket button on this or any solution. An email form will display to allow you to enter the details of the problem. This form will go directly to the EHR Product Support team.
