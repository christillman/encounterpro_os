Course Description: Referral Coordinator

Solution Title: Course Description: Referral Coordinator
Overview:

This course is designed for the referral coordinator or other office personnel who will maintain the list of consultants, edit and schedule referrals ordered by the providers, and monitor outstanding referrals.

Audience: Referral Coordinator
Prerequisites: 
-- encounterPRO Basics

Agenda:

  • Using Referrals in encounterPRO
  • EncounterPRO Tasks Typical for the Role
  • Maintaining the Consultants List
  • Editing and Scheduling Referrals
  • Monitoring Outstanding Referrals
  • Printing Referral Documents and Reports
