Concept: Closing vs. Canceling

Description
In encounterPRO closing means that the treatment or assessment or other item was valid and has now been discontinued; it should still appear in the patient's chart under closed items. Canceling an item means that it was not valid and should not appear in the patient's chart.

Automatically Closing Items
Histories and observations, such as the physical exam, that are documented during the current encounter will close automatically at the end of the encounter. Assessments can be configured to either close at the end of the encounter (vaccine assessments), close automatically after a specific period of time, or stay open until manually closed.

Treatments that are associated with an assessment are automatically closed when the associated assessment is closed unless the user specifically flags that treatment to remain open.
