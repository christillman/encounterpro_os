How to: Order a Lab - Lab Integration.

Purpose
The clinical staff will order the lab or test as they normally would in EncounterPRO. During the Collect step, the lab facility must be specified in order for the treatment to appear on the Daily Report for that facility.

Before You Begin
You need to know which lab the specimen or patient will be sent to. If you do not specify the lab during the collection step, the lab will not appear on the Daily Lab Results screen. You will need to find it on the Outstanding Labs/Tests list.

NOTE: This does NOT electronically submit the lab order to the lab facility (for example, Labcorp). You will need to continue to submit lab orders to the lab facility as you do now.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Office View, select the Collect or Refer Specimen service bar. The Collect screen appears.
  2. The clinical staff can do one of the following:
    • Collect the specimen in house and send out the specimen, 
      OR
    • Refer the patient to the lab for both collection and analysis
  3. If the specimen is collected in house, select (highlight) the Collected bar. Then select the bar to the right of Specimen Sent To. The Refer Patient for Treatment screen appears.
  4. If you are referring the patient to the lab, select the bar to the right of Patient Referred To: The Refer Patient for Treatment screen appears.
  5. In order for the lab to appear on the Daily Lab Results screen, you need to specify where the lab specimen or patient is being sent. Select the Select Other Consultant button and choose the lab facility to which you are sending the specimen.
  6. Enter the Specimen ID from the lab specimen label (if the specimen was collected in house).
  7. Select the billing code for the lab collection. Select Yes to bill for the specimen collection.
  8. Select the Finished button. The lab collection screen reappears. Select Finished. The lab will now appear on the Outstanding Labs and Tests list and the Daily Lab Results list for the selected lab facility.
