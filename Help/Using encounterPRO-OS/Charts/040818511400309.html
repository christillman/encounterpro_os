How to: Document a Secondary Insurance Carrier.

Purpose
The primary insurance carrier for a patient is imported from the practice management system. If a patient has a secondary carrier, that can be documented manually in EncounterPRO.

Before You Begin
The integration between your PMS and EncounterPRO may already bring the secondary insurance information over to the Patient Demographics screen. This depends upon the brand of PMS that you are using.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Office View, select the Charts tab. The Patient Search screen appears.
  2. Enter the search criteria for the patient name.
  3. Select the patient from the list of matching patients. The Patient Demographics screen appears.
  4. Select the Insurance tab. The primary insurance carrier imported from the PMS appears on this tab.
  5. Select Add Carrier. The Authorities screen appears.
  6. Select the secondary insurance carrier for the patient, then select OK.
  7. Select Finished. The Patient Search screen reappears.
