How to: Exit the Patient

Purpose
There may be times when you need to close an encounter in progress. Perhaps the patient was unable to wait or there was a problem completing the last service. Closing the encounter preserves all information documented for the encounter and moves the patient directly into the Checkout Room.

Before You Begin
If the encounter did not happen, there was a duplicate check-in or other problem, then you will want to Cancel that encounter rather than Close it. See Concept: Closing vs. Canceling and How to: Cancel an Encounter for details.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Office view, select the room where the patient appears.
  2. Select the patient name bar. A menu of options appears.
  3. Select Exit.
  4. Enter a reason for closing the encounter or select one from the list.
  5. Select OK. If there are unfinished services for the encounter, a message appears to warn you.
  6. Select Yes to close the encounter and send the patient to the Checkout Room.
