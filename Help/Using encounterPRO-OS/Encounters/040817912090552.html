How to: Move a Patient from One Room to Another

Purpose
At times it will be necessary to move a patient from an Exam room or the Tech station to another room. For example, if you use a separate Vitals station you will need to move the patient from the Vitals station to the Exam room. Or if you send a patient for an off-site treatment and expect them to return the same day, move the patient from the exam room to the Holding room, then back to an exam room when they return.

Before You Begin
The goal is to make the Office View reflect which patient is physically in each room in the real office.

NOTE: A patient CANNOT be moved back to the Waiting Room. If, in reality, the patient has been sent to the Waiting Room after vitals or some other step, you should move them to the Holding room in the EncounterPRO Office View.


Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Office View, select the exam room where the patient is currently located. You will see the patient name bar and any pending service bars.
  2. Select the patient name bar on the left. A menu of options appears.
  3. Select Change Room. A menu listing all the rooms available at your practice appears.
  4. Select the button that corresponds to the new room. The patient is "moved" to that room on the Office View.
