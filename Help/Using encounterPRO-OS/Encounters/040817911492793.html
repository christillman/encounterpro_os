How to: Bring a Patient Into a Treatment Room

Purpose
When it is time to bring a patient from the waiting room to a vitals station or exam room, you will need to "move" them to the corresponding station or room in encounterPRO.

Before You Begin
While it may seem a little backwards, you need to select the room you will be taking the patient to first, then select the patient from the Waiting Room list.

Details:

Steps
Follow the steps below:

  1. From the Office view, select the Waiting tab to review the number of patients in the waiting room, how long they have been waiting, which provider they are checked in to see, and the visit type. After reviewing the above information, decide which patient to bring to an exam room next.
  2. Select the Office tab to determine which stations or rooms are empty and decide where to put the patient. (If the room is Dirty, see Screen: How to: Clean a Dirty Treatment Room.)
  3. Select the empty room where the patient is to be seen, for example, Exam Room 1.
  4. Select the Get Patient button at the bottom of the screen. A list of patients currently in the Waiting Room appears.
  5. Select the desired patient from the list. You can select more than one if you need to put multiple patients, such as siblings, in the room together.

    NOTE: Even if only one patient appears on the list, you must select the patient name bar.
  6. Select Finished. The patient appears in the designated exam room. Depending on how the workplan was set-up, encounterPRO can automatically open to the patient Vitals screen. Otherwise, the exam room screen appears with the first service bar.
