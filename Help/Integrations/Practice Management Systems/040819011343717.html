Description
EncounterPRO EHR can be integrated with more than 35 different Practice Management Systems (PMS). The integration takes the form of:

  • New patient records are created in the PMS and automatically create new patient records in encounterPRO
  • Patient demographic data in the PMS is automatically created in encounterPRO*
  • Patients are checked in to the scheduling system and automatically appear in the Waiting Room of encounterPRO
  • Patient billing information is sent from encounterPRO to the PMS billing system when the patient reaches the Checkout Room in encounterPRO

* The exact data that is sent from the PMS to encounterPRO for the patient demographic data depends on the brand and version of your PMS.

It is important to note that the integration is basically a "one-way street." That is, information flows in one direction only. If patient demographic data is altered in encounterPRO, that information will be over-written by the information in the PMS the next time encounterPRO is contacted by the PMS. Changes to the billing information made in the PMS billing system will not reflected in encounterPRO.
