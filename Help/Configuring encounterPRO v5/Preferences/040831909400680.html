How to: Automatically Update Patient Referring Provider

Solution: If a patient is checked in with a Referring Provider specified, the practice may want EncounterPRO to automatically update the patient's 'Referring Provider' property to the consultant specified in the new encounter.  To have EncounterPRO automatically do this, set the General Preference 'Encounter Auto Update Patient Referring Provider' to 'Yes'.
