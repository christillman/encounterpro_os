    Description

    Preferences in encounterPRO can control various aspects of the system security, look & feel, and behavior. This screen allows the encounterPRO Administrators and Super Users to alter various preferences that can affect the entire practice, specific locations, specific computers, specific specialties, or individual users.

Details:

    How to Access This Screen
    Access this screen by selecting Configuration tab from the Office View, then selecting Preferences.
