Observations not displaying on the search screen

Cause: The observation is Simple where the screen is looking at Composite, or the observation is Composite where the screen is looking for Simple.
Solution: Changing c_treatment_type.composite_flag is how you change whether the Simple, Composite, or All button is highlighted on the observation search screen for a particular treatment type.  Simple is N, Composite is Y, and All is null/blank..
