Description
Treatment Types are the types of activities within encounterPRO directed to the diagnosis, prevention, or cure of a disease or injury.

The system divides treatments into types so that the different kinds of treatments can have different behaviors. For example, the screens used to order a prescription drug are very different than those used to order a follow-up visit. These behaviors are controlled by the treatment modes assigned to the treatment types.

Additionally, by dividing treatments into types it makes it easier for the provider to locate the desired treatment when creating the treatment plan for a specific patient.

Not all specialties will have all treatment types listed below.
