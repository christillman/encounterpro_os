How to: Configure medicaid or alternate billing

Solution:

To keep Medicaid codes from billing, go to c_authority and look at the coding_component_id field.  Delete just that field (not the row) anywhere you see JMJ_MEDICAID.

Where Medicaid billing well visit codes come from:

  1. Find the patient's insurance code (or authority_id) in p_patient_authority.authority_id
  2. Look for that authority_id in c_authority, then find the coding_component_id
  3. Find that component_id in c_component_attribute
  4. In the atttribute table, there should be a list_id_normal and a list_id_abnormal. T
  5. he lists for these are found in c_age_range_procedure, for their respective list_ids.
  6. The age ranges in c_age_range_procedure are linked to c_age_range
  7. The procedure_ids are found in c_procedure.
    • Remember, the prodecure_ids in c_age_range_procedure are NOT the cpt codes! They may be the same in some cases, but be SURE to double check in c_procedure!
